Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. General Information

The "Cafe Management App" ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our application for managing recipes and ingredients.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address - for registration, sign-in, and access recovery
  • Username - for identification in the system
  • User role - manager or employee to determine access rights

2.2 Application Data

  • Recipes - names, instructions, recipe photos
  • Ingredients - names, photos, units of measurement
  • Tags and categories - for organizing recipes
  • Portion sizes - serving sizes for each recipe
  • Ingredient quantities - for each portion size
  • User settings - preferences and configurations

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP address - for security and abuse prevention
  • Device information - device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage logs - for troubleshooting and app improvement

3. How We Use Data

  • Service provision - to operate the core app features
  • Authentication - for secure sign-in
  • Access management - to control access rights for managers and employees
  • App improvement - for usage analysis and optimization
  • Technical support - to resolve user issues
  • Security - to protect against fraud and abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase - a reliable cloud platform for data storage. All data is:

  • Stored in secure data centers
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Protected from unauthorized access
  • Regularly backed up

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • When necessary for service provision (e.g., hosting via Supabase)

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access - obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification - correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure - delete your data
  • Right to restriction - restrict data processing
  • Right to portability - receive data in a structured format

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide app services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Ensure security

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
